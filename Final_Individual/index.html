SCP Foundation - Secure, Contain, Protect.

Logged as: INVESTIGATOR-21

INVESTIGATOR-21, the following cases remain unresolved.


CASE 1: SCP-4971
Late one night, a security camera captured SCP-4971, a shadowy humanoid figure standing motionless at the edge of a rural forest. Its body appeared cloaked in a thick, mist-like substance, and its glowing, orb-like eyes were fixed on the camera. SCP-4971 has been reported to emit low-frequency whispers that induce paranoia in those nearby, causing them to feel watched even when alone. It vanishes into the fog when approached, leaving only strange claw marks etched into the ground. The creature is rarely seen but frequently felt, lingering on the edge of perception.


CASE 2: SCP-9236
In the early hours before dawn, SCP-9236 was spotted near an abandoned coastal village. Witnesses described it as a tall, skeletal figure with elongated limbs and translucent skin, revealing faintly glowing veins beneath. Its head lacked any discernible features, save for a wide, unsettling grin that never moved. SCP-9236 drifted silently along the shoreline, leaving no footprints, but the air around it felt cold and heavy. Electronic devices malfunctioned in its presence, and nearby wildlife fell eerily silent. When one witness attempted to approach, SCP-9236 vanished into a thick, unnatural fog, leaving behind an overwhelming sense of dread.


CASE 3: Sirenhead
On ██/██/20██, SCP-12432, known as "Sirenhead," was sighted by multiple witnesses in a secluded forest around 1900 hours. Described as a towering figure that blended seamlessly with the trees, its head resembled a rusted siren, emitting a low, rhythmic hum reminiscent of a distant signal. No aggressive behavior was observed, as the entity remained motionless and appeared to silently observe its surroundings. Locals later reported hearing familiar voices softly calling from the woods, although no source was identified. By dawn, Sirenhead had vanished, leaving behind an unsettling sense of being watched.


CASE 4: Mal0 Sightings
On ██/██/20██, a civilian reported a brief sighting of SCP-1471-A (MalO) after downloading the anomalous app linked to SCP-1471. Described as a tall, canine-like humanoid with black fur and glowing white eyes, the entity remained at the edge of the witness's peripheral vision, phasing in and out of reality without exhibiting hostile behavior. Though no physical traces were left behind, the witness experienced a lingering sense of being followed long after the sighting. Psychological effects and non-direct interaction with SCP-1471-A continue to warrant monitoring and further investigation.


CASE 5: SCP-239 Attack
SCP-239, also known as Chupacabra was sighted on a rural farm, where it attacked livestock with extreme aggression. The entity, described as a small, reptilian creature with glowing red eyes and spinal protrusions, tore through the herd of goats using its claws and fangs, leaving deep puncture wounds and exsanguinated bodies. The assault lasted approximately seven minutes before SCP-239 evaded capture, vanishing into nearby vegetation. No physical evidence was recovered, but the brutality of the attack was consistent with Chupacabra legend. Containment efforts are ongoing, with heightened security protocols in place.


CASE 6:
SCP-46-α (The Illusionist) and its child entity, SCP-46-β (The Nightmare), were encountered in an abandoned urban building. SCP-46-α manifested as a humanoid cloaked figure, manipulating the environment with disorienting illusions, while SCP-46-β, a shadowy mass, emerged to feed off fear induced by these distortions. SCP-46-β emitted a low-frequency sound, causing paralyzing fear and vivid hallucinations, leading to two fatalities from heart failure. The entities vanished before containment could be attempted, and their ability to manipulate perception and reality makes them highly dangerous. Enhanced protocols are now in effect.



ABOUT THE SCP FOUNDATION

Mankind in its present state has been around for a quarter of a million years, yet only a small fraction of that has been of any significance. So, what did we do for nearly 250,000 years? We huddled in caves and around small fires, fearful of the things that we didn't understand. It was more than explaining why the sun came up, it was the mystery of enormous birds with heads of men and rocks that came to life. So we called them "gods" and "demons", begged them to spare us, and prayed for salvation.
In time, their numbers dwindled and ours rose. The world began to make more sense when there were fewer things to fear, yet the unexplained can never truly go away, as if the universe demands the absurd and impossible. Mankind must not go back to hiding in fear. No one else will protect us, and we must stand up for ourselves. While the rest of mankind dwells in the light, we must stand in the darkness to fight it, contain it, and shield it from the eyes of the public, so that others may live in a sane and normal world.
